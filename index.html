Journey

CHARMS OF GREE​​CE A 
HEAVENLY CREATION

Day 1

Depart from KLIA or Changi Airport to Athens

Day 2

Arrival at Athens International Airport

 Discovering Athens Greece city

 Athens Acropolis Tour On Acropolis Hill, visualize the Golden Age of Athens; with the ruins of the Propylaea, Temple of Athena Nike and of course, the iconic Parthenon which still standing imposingly at the center of the ancient complex

  Visit the most impressive The Acropolis Museum

 ​Visit the Syntagma Square, Parliament House and Tomb of the Unknown Soldier, drive past Temple of the Olympian Zeus and stop at the Panathenaic Stadium

Day 3

Athens – Mykonos by flight from Athens to Mykonos Island 

 ​Discovery Admire the iconic Windmills, drop in Little Venice with restaurants and bars built right at the edge of the cliffs with balconies overhanging the water for some leisure time

 ​Exploring Manto Mavrogenous Square , Matoyianni shopping street. The rest of day is pretty much free at leisure, kicking back and enjoying the vibrant nightlife of Mykonos

Day 4

Mykonos- Santorini

 ​​Be charmed by the gleaming whitewashed houses, incredible blue skies and deep blue sparkling sea. In the afternoon, Board a high speed ferry to Santorini in less than 3 hours

PRIVATE YATCH

Day 5

Santorini-Private Luxury Sunset exclusive cruise tour

 Sail below the Caldera and gaze at the picturesque scenery

 ​Eat a delicious barbecue prepared on board

  Sip on unlimited drinks including refreshing white wine, beers, and soft drinks. Explore the island of Santorini from the sea and visit areas that you can not reach by car

Day 6

Santorini Island tour

 ​​Thanks to the rich volcanic soil, Santorini is renowned for its local wine varieties as it holds a huge tradition in wine making. Grab a chance to enjoy wine tasting with lunch at one of the finest Volcano Winery

 ​​After lunch in Fira, drive past the quaint village of Pyrgos on the way up to the highest point, Profitis Ilias. Enjoy the beautiful black sands beach. Oia Sunset Watch In the afternoon, proceed to the most famous village in Santorini—Oia, located at the northern tip of the island for the world-famous sunset watch. Marvel at the typical Santorinian architecture with attractive domes and whitewashed walls that blends perfectly with the clear blue skies and the Aegean Sea

Day 7

Domestic flight from Santorini to Athens

 ​Free at leisure day

Day 8

Flight back to Singapore / Kuala Lumpur

Day 9

Arrive home with sweet memories
